Welcome to my world. Where data science meets sustainability!

I'm Matteo Jucker Riva, a data scientist and geography PhD with over 10 years of experience making data work for people all over the world. My passion is building tools that help folks from all walks of life make informed decisions—especially when it comes to our planet's sustainability challenges.

My data science toolkit spans machine learning and deep learning, with a focus on applying computer vision to GIS geospatial data. I develop solutions like time series forecasting models and CNNs for satellite image classification. I also design, maintain, and enrich large-scale environmental and land-use databases, crucial for monitoring, carbon accounting, and impact analysis.

"Making complex environmental data accessible and actionable is my ultimate goal."

Beyond data science, I'm an environmental studies expert with hands-on field experience: deploying sensors, calibrating instruments, and leading field sampling campaigns across diverse ecosystems. I've worked on regenerative and precision agriculture, dry forest and rangeland management, applying satellite remote sensing (NDVI, Sentinel, Landsat) and GIS modeling to monitor everything from soil health to carbon sequestration.

I also love the human side of this work—leading workshops, teaching, and building capacity among stakeholders in environmental governance. Whether advising on regenerative agriculture, developing climate models, or training local communities, my goal is the same: making complex environmental data accessible and actionable.
